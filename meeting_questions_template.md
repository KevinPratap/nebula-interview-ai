# Meeting Questions Template

Use these questions before, during, and after meetings to drive better outcomes. Saved alongside your Nebula meeting notes.

---

## Pre-Meeting (Prepare)

- What is the **goal** of this meeting? (Decide, inform, brainstorm?)
- What **decisions** need to be made?
- Who needs to be **heard** on each topic?
- What **data** do I need to have ready?
- What **outcome** would make this meeting a success?
- What **questions** will I likely be asked?

---

## During Meeting (Ask)

### For clarity
- "Can you help me understand the **constraint** behind that?"
- "What does **success** look like for this?"
- "Who is the **decision maker** on this?"
- "What have we **tried before** that didn't work?"
- "What **assumptions** are we making?"

### For decisions
- "What's the **tradeoff** we're accepting here?"
- "By when do we need to **decide**?"
- "What's the **fallback** if this doesn't work?"
- "Who needs to **sign off** on this?"
- "What's the **cost of waiting**?"

### For action items
- "Who **owns** this?"
- "What's the **deadline**?"
- "What **blockers** do you anticipate?"
- "How will we **measure** completion?"
- "Who needs to be **informed**?"

### For disagreement
- "What would it take to **change your mind**?"
- "What **evidence** would settle this?"
- "Can we **experiment** our way to an answer?"
- "What's the **worst case** if we go with option B?"

---

## Post-Meeting (Review)

- Did we achieve the **stated goal**?
- What **decisions** were made? (log them)
- What **action items** came out? (owner + deadline)
- What **surfaced unexpectedly**?
- Who needs a **follow-up**?
- What should I **prepare** for next time?
- **Rating** (1-5): Was this meeting worth the time?

---

## Role-Specific Questions

### For Interviews
- "What does a typical **day** look like in this role?"
- "What **metrics** define success for this position?"
- "What's the **biggest challenge** the team faces right now?"
- "How does this team **collaborate** with other teams?"
- "What **growth opportunities** exist?"

### For Client Meetings
- "What **changed** since we last spoke?"
- "What's the **priority** this week?"
- "Is there anything **blocking** your team?"
- "How does this align with your **quarterly goals**?"
- "What **feedback** do you have on our work?"

### For 1:1s
- "What's **going well** since last time?"
- "What's **frustrating** you?"
- "How can I **support** you better?"
- "What do you want to **learn** next?"
- "How are you **feeling** about the project?"

### For Standups
- "What did I **accomplish** since yesterday?"
- "What am I **working on** today?"
- "What **blockers** do I have?"
- "Do I need **help** from anyone?"
- "What **deadlines** are approaching?"
